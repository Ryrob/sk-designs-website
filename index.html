About Us

Where High-End Design Meets Structural Excellence

S&K Designs is a locally owned, family-operated business dedicated to providing Utah homeowners with reliable, professional service and superior results.

We specialize in bridging the gap between high-end design and structural excellence. We apply the same level of precision to framing, electrical, and basement finishing that we do to custom cabinetry.

From the first sketch to the final coat of finish, S&K Designs is committed to building spaces that last a lifetime.

What We Do

Our Services

From intricate woodworking details to full-scale construction, we deliver craftsmanship at every level.

Custom Built-Ins

Handcrafted cabinetry, entertainment centers, and media walls tailored precisely to your space. Every detail is designed for both beauty and function.

Basement Finishing

Turn-key basement transformations including framing, electrical, and high-volume drywall with a focus on structural integrity and fire safety.

Outdoor Structures

Expertly designed and built pergolas, timber frames, garden structures, and outdoor living spaces that extend your home into the outdoors.

Specialized Woodworking

Unique geometric accent walls, custom wood features, handcrafted furniture, and one-of-a-kind pieces that serve as the focal point of any room.
